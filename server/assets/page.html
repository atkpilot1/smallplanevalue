A note on GA aircraft valuations
Unlike real estate, GA aircraft sale prices are not publicly recorded anywhere. SmallPlaneValue.com shows asking price ranges pulled from active listings (Trade-A-Plane, Controller, Barnstormers) paired with category-specific discount bands that represent typical negotiated outcomes — based on broker experience, not verified transactions. We believe being honest about this is more useful than showing made-up "sale prices."
